**Formula:**
$$
\begin{align}
& - \ \ \ \ xy\ +\  \overline{x}z\ +\ yz\ = \ xy\ +\ \overline{x}z \\ \\
& - \ \ \ \ (x+y) \cdot(\overline{x}+z)\cdot(y+z)= (x+y)\cdot(\overline{x}z) \\
\end{align}
$$

**Dimostrazione:**
$$ 
{\displaystyle {\begin{alignedat}{3}xy+{\bar {x}}z+yz&=xy+{\bar {x}}z+yz(x+{\bar {x}})\\
&=xy+{\bar {x}}z+xyz+{\bar {x}}yz\\
&=xy+xyz+{\bar {x}}z+{\bar {x}}yz\\
&=xy(1+z)+{\bar {x}}z(1+y)\\
&=xy(1)+{\bar {x}}z(1)\\
&=xy+{\bar {x}}z\\
\end{alignedat}}}
$$
